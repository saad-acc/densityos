Problem · 02

Contention limits throughput in multi-tenant infrastructure.

Four failure modes, all driven by the same root cause — competing workloads hitting the same resources at microsecond precision.

Fault · 01

Capacity sits idle for safety.

Workload collisions degrade performance. You maintain buffer capacity to prevent interference — cores you bought but cannot bill.

Symptom

When Customer A's workload peaks at the same moment as Customer B's, both experience slowdowns. Operations solves this by leaving 30%+ headroom — cores that generate zero revenue but prevent SLA breaches.

Safe to run at 88% utilisation. DensityOS prevents timing collisions by governing when workloads execute. Same hardware, no interference, 20 points more capacity you can bill.

0% contention-driven failures. DensityOS staggers colliding requests by microseconds, eliminating failures at the resource level.
